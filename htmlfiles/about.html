About Samatva

Samatva is a gender-responsive support platform dedicated to empowering individuals facing domestic violence, harassment, and social injustice. We believe that knowledge of rights, access to counselors, and legal guidance can transform lives.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, reliable, and compassionate support for survivors of violence, ensuring they know their rights and have the tools to act with confidence.

Our Vision

A society where every individual lives free from fear, empowered by knowledge, and supported by strong legal and social systems.

Our Values

  • Empowerment through knowledge
  • Compassion and confidentiality
  • Accessibility and inclusivity
  • Justice and equality
