Know your rights. Act with confidence.

Key Indian rights and laws protecting against domestic violence, harassment, exploitation, and abuse.

Your Rights

Right to Protection

You have the right to immediate protection from violence and abuse.

Right to Shelter

Safe housing and shelter homes are guaranteed under law.

Right to Legal Aid

Free legal aid ensures justice and fair representation.

Applicable Laws

Domestic Violence Act, 2005

Provides protection orders, residence orders, monetary relief, custody, and compensation.

IPC Section 498A

Addresses cruelty by husband or relatives, including harassment for dowry.

Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

Prohibits giving/taking of dowry. Harassment for dowry is punishable.

POSH Act, 2013

POCSO Act, 2012
